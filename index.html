About Nahida

Nahida is the Dendro Archon and the God of Wisdom in Genshin Impact. She is a member of the Sages of Sumeru and is known for her wisdom and knowledge. Nahida is also known as the "God of Dreams" and has the ability to manipulate dreams and memories. She is a kind and gentle person, but can also be fierce when it comes to protecting her people.

  • Skillsets: Dream Manipulation, Wisdom Sharing, Memory Reading, Dendro Elemental Control, Illusion Creation, Telepathy, Purification, Knowledge Bestowal

Nahida Profile

Rukkhadevata, the Original Dendro Archon

Ancient Sumeru
Era of the Archon War (~2000+ years ago)

Rukkhadevata, the Greater Lord, was the original Dendro Archon and founder of Sumeru's civilization, revered for her wisdom and guidance.

Sacrifice during the Cataclysm

Irminsul, Sumeru
~500 years ago

Rukkhadevata sacrificed herself to save Irminsul and Sumeru from the corruption of the Cataclysm, erasing her own existence from the world.

Birth of Nahida (Lesser Lord Kusanali)

Sanctuary of Surasthana
After the Cataclysm (~500 years ago)

Nahida was born from the pure branch of Irminsul as the new Dendro Archon, inheriting the legacy of Rukkhadevata.

Confined in Sanctuary of Surasthana

Sumeru City
For centuries

Despite being the new Archon, Nahida was kept isolated by the Akademiya, with the people of Sumeru knowing her only as a distant deity.

Becomes the God of Wisdom

Sumeru
Present Day

After the events of the Sumeru Archon Quest, Nahida is recognized as the true God of Wisdom, guiding Sumeru with compassion and intellect, and forging her own path as the Dendro Archon.
